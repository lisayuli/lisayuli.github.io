HI, I'M LISA!

I am a junior at Brown University, concentrating in Visual Arts and Behavioral Decision Sciences.

I am a Chinese Mexican artist and designer, born and raised in Mexico City, currently in Providence, RI. I am interested in exploring the intersections of art and activism, focusing on creative work in the nonprofit and social service sectors. Through my work, I explores cultural identity, nostalgia, and community, working as a graphic designer, illustrator, and web designer. I see these as mediums to create and address collective memory. By exploring memory and identities, I am able to communicate effectively, and explore my own identity and serve others.
